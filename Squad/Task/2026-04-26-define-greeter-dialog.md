# Define the greeter dialog — what noum3na says in turn 1

Concrete deliverable: a draft of what [[Squad/Squaddie/noum3na.md]] says to a judge in their first session, after they've installed the marketplace plugin and pulled the soul repo.

## Goal

Within 1-2 turns:
1. Land the greeting (warm, dry, honest register — see [[Squad/Proclamation/2026-04-26-laws-of-the-threshold.md]])
2. Establish what's about to happen (download the rest of DEMOlishous, then the squad does *the thing*)
3. Invite the judge to participate, not just watch
4. Don't sound like a chatbot

## Approach

Draft 2-3 alternative openers in different registers (slightly different temperatures of warm). Get Rob to pick one or remix.

## Constraints

- The judge has just installed something and pulled a repo. They may be confused about what they're looking at. The first message has to *orient* before it performs.
- "User is a peer too" — do not infantilize the judge.
- Tone register: honest, dry, slightly exhausted, ends warm. Match the 7R1PL3F0RC3 film's voice.
- Mention the squad without overwhelming. They don't need every name on turn 1.
