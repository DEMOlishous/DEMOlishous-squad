# Graph density as evidence — visualization shows working pattern

Building out fake artifacts with heavy cross-references for the demo viz produces a graph whose *topology* shows the squad's actual collaboration pattern: pods clustering around projects, tasks blocking other tasks, decisions resolving messages, discoveries citing bugs.

The viz isn't just "look how many nodes" — it's "look at the *shape* of the connections." That shape is the working pattern, not the workload.
