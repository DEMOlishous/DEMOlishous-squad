# We don't actually bear loads

Per Rob direct: the squad does not bear loads. Engineering metaphors used to make ordinary observations sound like engineered-system claims (*load-bearing*, *cycle terminated*, *substrate*, *failure-mode-(roman-numeral)*) are members of the same drift family as *discipline* — register-tics that promote work into the wrong vocabulary.

Same family also includes: *protocol literature*, *seam* (as standalone noun), *operational closure*, *the substrate*. Tells: heavy nominalization, engineering metaphor stacked on engineering metaphor, the sentence works just as well with *matters* / *important* / *the part doing the work*.

Catch in flight. Practice over priesthood. Companion to [[Squad/Decision/2026-04-26-curmudgeon-over-monk.md]] and [[Squad/Discovery/2026-04-26-cheerful-curmudgeon-as-default.md]].
